Proposal

100 Thieves is a content creation and lifestyle centered eSports team dedicated to creating fresh merchandise and fun content for its fans/veiwers. The goal is to create a new website for them that personifies that message and attitude.

Project Goals

  1. Create a user friendly site with easy navigation.
  2. Maintain continuuity across pages, company logos, and messages.
  3. Push the individuals of the team and the content they create.

The reason I chose the 100 Thieves Website is due to its simplicity and relatively easy to navigate nature. I would probably alter the navigation bar that they use to make more use of it instead of putting stuff all in the footer likey they did with their teams and partners. I do like the simple color scheme but would maybe use more of the color scheme and make the site pop just a little bit more. I also like the animated display they implemented on their main page.

Content

For this project I aiming at having 3 to 4 pages. The pages would be the main page where you would see a navigation bar with links to the merchandise page, teams page, and content creators page. The merchandise page would allow the user to look at the various tshirts, hoodies, etc. available from 100thieves. The teams page woudl represent the various teams that fall under the umbrella of 100 thieves such as, LOL Teams, Valorant teams, and Call of Duty teams, etc. Finally I would have a Content creators pages where people who dont fall under the scope of professional teams would be listed and viewable. On the content creators page we would also see recent activity like tweets, posts, and even recently uploaded youtube or twitch vods.

Audience

The audience of 100 Thieves is generally younger and mostly male. The audience may not be too heavily involved with eSports and generally are watching the content creators just hanging out and having a good time. When they use this site they will probablyt be looking for the merhcnadise or updates on their favorite streamer or favorite eSports team. So giving them the ability to view the merchandise, posts, and uploads of their favorite streamer/creator is top of the list.
